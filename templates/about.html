About Us

We are a group of 4 students in our 2nd year in software engineering,from Sami Shamoon College of Engineering,that worked under the supervision and guidance of D"r Chasidim Hadas and Cochavi Hadar to create this project.

From March 2020 the corona virus struck the country of israel and since then a lot of small businesses were hurt economically and still are,due to the lockdowns and fear from the virus.

Our project goal is helping this small businesses to get througth the corona virus pandemic by opening their own digital store for free and by hellping to connect them and customers in a safer way,without the need to fear the virus.

We believe that every store owner should get help during these hard times,no matter is race, sex,gender, religion and nationality.
